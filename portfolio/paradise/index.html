Tourist Attraction

WOLMI THEME PARK
An amusement park located in Wolmido, Jung-gu, Incheon. Following My Land established in 1992, this amusement park opened around November 2009.
ARABETGIL
The first inland canal in Korea opened in 2012, Arabaetgil connects the downstream of Han River and the Yellow Sea with an 18-km waterway.
BMW DRIVING CENTER
The BMW Driving Center consists of tracks, exhibition halls, event halls, junior campuses, and comfortable seating areas to enjoy driving under the theme of experience, fun and green.
GOLF PARK
A golf club located 15 minutes from Paradise City, SKY72 Golf & Resort spans 4 million square meters with 72 holes.
SINPO INTERNATIONAL MARKET
The first modern market in Incheon, Sinpo International Market is Incheon's most famous market with a history of over 100 years along with the opening of Incheon Port.
SEASIDE PARK
Seaside Park is a park located in Yeongjong Sky City, Jung-gu, Incheon, and opened on July 1, 2016.
EURWANGNI BEACH
Only 15 minutes away from Paradise City, Eurwangni Beach boasts a beautiful view at sunset. With a 700-meter long sandy beach and waters about 1.5 meters deep on average, it is famous for its scenic beauty with lush pine groves and lava rocks lining its sides.
